# Carpe Diem — Seize the day

**Carpe Diem** is a calm, pocket-based wish list for moments you don’t want to forget — trips, outings, treats, goals, and “someday” ideas — organised your way.

## Built around how you actually think

- **Pockets** group wishes (travel, weekends, treats, adventures) so lists stay approachable.
- **Planning tags** — time, budget, energy, company, distance, transport, and more — help you skim what fits today without opening every card.
- **Places** tie wishes to real locations when you want maps and directions handy.
- **Local-first**: your wishes and planning data stay on **your device** (with optional reminders on your phone).

## Gentle nudges, not noise

Thoughtful reminders and optional inspiration pings are designed to help you notice ideas at the right time — without turning your wishes into another inbox.

## Privacy-minded by design

We don’t run a central “wish cloud” readout of everything you capture. Details are covered in our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).
