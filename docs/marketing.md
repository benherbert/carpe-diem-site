# Carpe Diem Wish List - Seize the day

**About me.** I’m Ben - I have ADHD. Sometimes I’m overwhelmed by all the ideas and things I want to do; time slips away, I forget to act, and I end up disappointed. So I made an app that works for **me**: I can add wishes quickly and find them again without fighting the list. I’m less likely to miss out now - **I hope it helps you, too.**

**Carpe Diem** is a calm, pocket-based wish list for moments you don’t want to forget - trips, outings, treats, goals, and "someday" ideas - organised your way.

## Built around how you actually think

- **Pockets** group wishes (travel, weekends, treats, adventures) so lists stay approachable.
- **Planning tags** - time, budget, energy, company, distance, transport, and more - help you skim what fits today without opening every card.
- **Places** tie wishes to real locations when you want maps and directions handy.
- **Pinning** keeps the pockets you use most at hand so favourites don’t disappear under a long list.
- **Local-first**: your wishes and planning data stay on **your device** (with optional reminders on your phone).

## ADHD-friendly pacing

Long "someday" lists can feel noisy or paralysing. Carpe Diem is designed to **lower overwhelm**: smaller surfaces (pockets, filters, tags) instead of one giant backlog; metadata that matches real-world decisions ("do I have energy for this?" "how long is it?"); optional nudges toward ideas you’ve already captured - not another endless feed. It won’t replace clinical support, but it may fit how **ADHD and similar brains** prefer to plan in the moment.

## Accessibility (a11y)

We work toward **strong accessibility** on iOS and Android: assistive technologies such as **VoiceOver** and **TalkBack**, meaningful labels, adequate touch targets on key actions, and layouts that don’t rely on colour alone. Accessibility is **ongoing** - if a flow is hard to use with your setup, we want to hear about it via [Support](support.md).

## Gentle nudges, not noise

Thoughtful reminders and optional inspiration pings are designed to help you notice ideas at the right time - without turning your wishes into another inbox.

## Privacy-minded by design

We don’t run a central "wish cloud" readout of everything you capture. Details are covered in our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).
